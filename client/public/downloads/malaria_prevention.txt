SMART HEALTH NORTHEAST - MALARIA PREVENTION STEPS

CAUTIONS:
1. Malaria and Dengue risk increases significantly after floods due to stagnant water.
2. Symptoms include high fever, shivering, and headache. Do not ignore them.

PREVENTION STEPS:
1. ELIMINATE BREEDING SITES: Empty, dry, covers, or dispose of containers that hold water (tyres, buckets, flower pots).
2. USE NETS: Sleep under Insecticide-Treated Bed Nets (ITNs) every night.
3. REPELLENTS: Apply insect repellent on exposed skin, especially during dawn and dusk.
4. CLOTHING: Wear long-sleeved shirts and long pants to minimize skin exposure.

TREATMENT:
Diagnosis and treatment for Malaria are free at all government health centers. Seek help immediately if fever persists.
